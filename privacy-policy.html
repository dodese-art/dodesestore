1. Introduction

DodeseStore ("we", "our", "us") operates as an AI-generated wall art business. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you interact with our platform integrations, including Etsy and Pinterest.

2. Information We Collect

Through authorized API integrations, we may access: public listing data, order fulfillment information, and aggregated analytics provided by platform APIs. We do not collect personal information beyond what is necessary to fulfill orders.

3. How We Use Information

Information accessed via platform APIs is used solely to: manage product listings, process and fulfill orders, and analyze business performance. We do not use this data for advertising or profiling purposes.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer any information to third parties. Data accessed through platform APIs is not shared outside of DodeseStore's internal operations.

5. Third-Party Platforms

Our integrations with Etsy and Pinterest are governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service. We access only the data explicitly authorized under each platform's API terms.

6. Pinterest API

DodeseStore uses the Pinterest API to manage its business profile and publish product pins. DodeseStore is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Pinterest, Inc. Any reference to Pinterest within this application solely identifies the third-party service being accessed and does not imply any association or endorsement by Pinterest.

When a user disconnects the DodeseStore application from their Pinterest account, all associated Pinterest data — including access tokens and any data obtained through the Pinterest API — is permanently and immediately deleted from our systems. No Pinterest user data is retained following disconnection.

Data obtained through the Pinterest API, as well as any content derived from such data, is used exclusively for DodeseStore's internal business operations. Such data is not sold, licensed, redistributed, or otherwise transferred to any third party under any circumstance.

7. Data Retention

We retain order-related data only as long as necessary for business operations and legal compliance, typically no longer than 3 years.

8. Security

We implement standard security practices to protect any data we access, including secure API authentication and limited data access principles.
